Getting Started

Pokemon are legendary creatures that can be trained, caught and played with. Although when trained, they can be very nice, they can also be very dangerous. In the Pokemon movies, Ash starts out with a basic Pokemon, Pikachu. Pikachu proves to be dangerous and he does not like Ash, until he turns around and saves Pikachus life. There are tons of Pokemon and tons of Pokemon types.

Pokemon types These are all the 10 Pokemon types: Fire Water Electric Ice Ground Rock Earth Psychic Darkness Dragon Fairy Grass Steel Poison Bug Flying Normal Fighting
